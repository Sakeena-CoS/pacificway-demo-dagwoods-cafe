The Dagwoods Difference

Langford's lunch spot.

At 133-662 Goldstream Avenue in Langford, Dagwoods Café has earned its reputation as the go-to spot for a great sandwich. Our 468 Facebook fans know why — we take the time to make every order fresh, use quality ingredients, and deliver real flavour every visit.

  • Made fresh to order — every sandwich is made when you order it, never sitting pre-made in a case.
  • Locally sourced ingredients — we prioritise local suppliers for the freshest produce and meats.
  • Quick lunch service — we know you're on a lunch break, so we work fast without cutting corners on quality.
  • Vegetarian options — plenty of plant-based choices so everyone at the table is well fed.
  • Dine-in & takeout — eat here or grab it to go, we make both experiences seamless.
